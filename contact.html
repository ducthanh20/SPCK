What to Write to Me About
  • Questions about family travel.
  • Proposals for partnering, per my Work With Me page.
  • If you’ve found something amazing, unique and kid-friendly somewhere in the world, please let me know!
  • If you represent a destination, hotel, travel product/service or PR firm and you want me to be aware of you, great! I only recommend places and things that we have personally tried out and loved. I’m always looking for new kid-friendly destination ideas.
What Not to Write to Me About
  • Guest posts. This is my own personal travel blog.
  • Sponsored posts. Your “article” with casino/vaping links isn’t exactly a perfect fit for my site.
  • Link placement/swaps. I’m not going to add your post link or site link to an existing post.
  • Offers for unpaid affiliate/commission/revenue-sharing partnerships.
  • Requests to plan and book your RTW trip. I’m not a travel planner or travel agent
  • Requests to review your app/website/product/song/ebook/Kickstarter campaign. There are a million great ideas out there and I unfortunately don’t have time to review everything that people send me.
